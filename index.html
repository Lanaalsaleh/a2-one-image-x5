Distorted Perfection

Inspired by The Glitch Studies Manifesto’s idea that technology is never completely perfect, this section uses scaling, contrast, rotation, translation, blur, and repetition to subtly distort the original image without fully destroying it. The image remains recognizable, reflecting how glitches expose imperfections rather than hide them.
